Idahoans Hope for Spring

Thelma D. Ware

Spring is a nice warm welcome to Idahoans after the long and cold winter. The snow is finally melted, and they can go back to their usual sunny activities such as hiking and spending weekends on the shores of the lake. Though the weather in Preston can be unpredictable and variable, such snowfall as late as March and rain and sunshine into April, as spring unfolds, the community brightens because of the upcoming Festivals that occur throughout the spring months leading into summer. One of the biggest events that occurs in Preston is the Easter Egg Scramble in Preston City Park. During the Easter Scramble, there are four scheduled egg hunts which will provide plenty of enjoyment for children of all ages. There will also be a special visit from the Easter Bunny.
