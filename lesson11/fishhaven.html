Spring at Bear Lake

Thelma D. Ware

As spring makes its way to Fish Haven, early leaves and flowers welcome you as you ride or walk a lake or mountain trail. Take a ride on the Bear Trail-Road Bike Route along highway 30 for scenic views. This ride features spectacular mountain views and glimpses of Bear Lake. The paved trails make this an easy ride for anyone in your family. Along the 4.5 mile route, there are also numerous amenities along the route for camping, eating, and recreation. But be sure to take a dip in the turquoise blue waters of Bear Lake, aka the "Caribbean of the Rockies," at one of the many beaches whenever you need a break!
