The primary text fonts are Comfortaa and Montserrat.

Comfortaa will be used for heading text, like the site header, h1, and h2, because it is slightly more stylized and matches the logo font. Montserrat will be used for navigation, footer, and paragraph text because it is more readable than Comfortaa.
