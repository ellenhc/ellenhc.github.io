Site Name

The website is going to be called lifeweather.us. This name emphasizes the site's purpose to provide local weather and news.

This domain name is available for $12 according to Google Domains.

Site Purpose

The purpose of the Life Weather website is to provide local weather and news information for a specific location. This purpose will be met by sharing current weather stories, future weather conditions, and related news content with website visitors. The goal is to become a trusted resource for the community by providing such information promptly and accurately. To accomplish this goal, the website will be responsive to meet visitor needs regardless of their screen size.
