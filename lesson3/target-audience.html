Target Audience

Description of the target audience.

Personas

Persona 1:

Name Janet
Job Title Librarian
Demographics
  • 58 years old
  • Married
  • Mother of 2 adult children
  • Master of Library Science (MSLS)
Goals Janet tries to walk during her lunch break everyday. She checks the outside temperature to decide how many layers to put on, and checks the weather to see if she needs an umbrella or not. In winter, the library closes during snow storms so Janet needs to know approximately how long the storm will last.
Environment Janet is comfortable working with computers when sticking to the basics. She primarily uses a desktop or mobile phone for interet browsing.

Persona 2:

Name Matthew
Job Title Computer Engineer
Demographics
  • 27 years old
  • Married without kids
  • Bachelor's
  • CS Grad student
Goals Matt rides the company bus to work. Throughout the year, he needs to know what the day's weather conditions are to dress and prepare appropriately for his commute. In the winter, he also needs to know if the bus will be running during inclement weather.
Environment Matt is very familiar with computers and has created his own websites before. He uses desktops, laptops, tablets, and cell phones to browse the web, although he most often uses his phone.

Scenarios

  • Should I take my raincoat with me to work today?
  • How warmly should I dress to be comfortable for my walk?
  • I need to know when the weather will be clear enough to paint my front door.
  • What clothing should I pack for my vacation in another state?
  • How many days is the winter storm expected to last?
