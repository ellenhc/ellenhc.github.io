About

World-class, whitewater rafting experiences on the Salmon River

Salmon River Adventures is a river rafting tour company that offers day and overnight rafting trips for groups or individuals along the Salmon River.

Being located in Riggins, Idaho (about three hours north of Boise) means we're truly deep in the middle of Idaho's wilderness and surrounded by scenic views.

You'll always receive top-notch service, safety, and care when rafting with us. Our staff have many years of rafting experience and conduct each trip with state of the art equipment that complies with all regulations.

Not interested in a guided rafting trip? We also offer additional services.

Rentals

Rent an inflatable kayak (1-2 passengers) or sport raft (4-8 passengers) for half or full-day fun. Call 1-800-456-7890 for more details.

Self-Guided Trips

Want to raft the river without a guide? We offer mapped routes for self-guided trips including shuttle drop off and pick up for maximum ease.

Camping Sites

Stay nearby at one of our tent or RV sites. Our private campgrounds have power and water hook ups, bathrooms, and easy river access.
