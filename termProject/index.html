Our recent expedition with Salmon River Adventures was by far one of the best rafting trips ever. The office staff were organized and efficient. The safety briefing was structured, thorough, and made sure all the first-time rafters in our group received the necessary information. Our guide, Megan, was knowledgeable, funny and made our day on the beautiful Salmon river even that much better. We will definitely be back for a trip next summer.

Melissa Ward

The first whitewater rafting company I ventured out with set a high bar, but Salmon River Adventures met and passed that measure. James took us out and was very skilled, personable, and fun river guide. My friend, a first-time rafter, perfectly handled class IV-V rapids thanks to James. If you like adrenaline rushes, you’ve got to ride the Salmon river! Thankfully, there are also hiking trails for those who chose to opt out of the advanced rapids.

John Mitchell

I highly recommend Salmon River Adventures if you are new to whitewater rafting. My siblings and I took our dad for Father’s Day and were a little nervous beforehand. However, when approaching more dangerous rapids, Megan pulled the raft aside to make sure everyone understood the instructions. That helped us newbies safely navigate the rapids without panicking. I think this experience just jump-started a new hobby for me.

Emily Scott
