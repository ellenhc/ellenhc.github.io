Want to become a guide?

We currently have both part-time and full-time positions open. If you're committed to becoming a professional river guide for Salmon River Adventures, we offer two whitewater raft guide training programs in April and June. Check out the current rafting news on the homepage for starting dates. But please remember, all dates are tentative based upon river water levels and conditions.

All training programs occur over four weekends and cover the State of Idaho Outfitters and Guides Licensing requirements. Such requirements include raft flipping, first aid & CPR, swimming in rapids, and much more. By signing up, you will receive a training manual and a season pass to join any scheduled trips during that season. Give us a call, send an email, or complete our contact form to join a training program.
